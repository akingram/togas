Our Story of Excellence

For over a decade, Togas has been synonymous with purity, quality, and authenticity in groundnut oil production.

From Farm to Table

Founded in 2010, Togas began with a simple mission: to provide Nigerian families with pure, unadulterated groundnut oil that retains all its natural goodness.

What started as a small family business has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted groundnut oil brands, serving both retail customers and industrial clients.

Our commitment to quality has never wavered. We maintain traditional extraction methods while implementing modern quality control systems to ensure every bottle meets our high standards.

Our Journey

2010: Founded with one small processing unit
2014: Expanded to industrial production
2018: Launched AfriReach industrial division
2020: ISO certification achieved
2023: Serving 5 African countries
