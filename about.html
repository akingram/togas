About Togas Edible Oil NG Ltd

Togas Edible Oil NG Ltd is a Nigerian-owned food manufacturing company committed to producing high-quality, affordable, and nutritious edible oils.

We combine modern refining processes with strict quality control to deliver oils that are safe, healthy, and reliable for daily use in Nigerian homes and businesses.
