Togas Natural Palm Oil

Unrefined, antioxidant-rich palm oil that brings authentic flavour to traditional Nigerian dishes. Its deep reddish-orange colour and natural aroma enhance soups, stews and fried delicacies.

  • 100% natural, unrefined – no bleaching
  • High in beta-carotene (Vitamin A) & Vitamin E
  • Perfect for Egusi, Oha, Ofe Akwu & more
  • Authentic palm kernel aroma & taste
  • Free from chemicals & preservatives
  • Available in 1L, 3L, 5L, 10L & 20L containers
  • Cold-pressed to retain natural nutrients
